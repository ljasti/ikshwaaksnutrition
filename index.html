Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Amrot and our products

What is Amrot?

Amrot is our product family of clean, nutrient-dense foods that includes Microgreens, Protein Powders, Mushrooms, and a Health Drink Mix — each crafted to support balanced daily nutrition with essential macros and micronutrients.

How do I consume Amrot?

Use Amrot items as follows: Microgreens as fresh toppers and in salads, Protein Powders in shakes or porridges, Mushrooms in everyday cooking, and the Health Drink Mix stirred into warm water.

Is Amrot suitable for children and seniors?

Yes. Amrot products are designed as family-friendly daily nutrition. For toddlers or specific dietary needs, consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.

Does Amrot contain eggs, maida, or refined sugar?

No. Amrot products contain no eggs, no maida, and no refined sugar. They are crafted without added oils or preservatives.

Can Amrot replace milk?

Amrot offers clean daily nutrition compared to adulterated milk. The Health Drink Mix can be consumed with water; other Amrot items complement a balanced diet.

Is Amrot vegan?

Amrot items are plant-based. Prepared with water, the Health Drink Mix fits a vegan-friendly routine.

Is Amrot more than milk?

Yes. Amrot Health Drink Mix is a premium superfood blend created with multi-grains, millets, pulses, seeds, and nuts. It is a safe, wholesome alternative to adulterated milk and delivers complete daily nutrition across carbohydrates, proteins, fiber, healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Who can consume Amrot?

Amrot is suitable for all age groups. It supports energy, immunity, strength, and digestion, making it a family-friendly daily nutrition choice.

What are the key ingredient categories?

Amrot Health Drink Mix (Protein Power) brings together 25 natural ingredients:

  • Pulses: Brown Chana (Desi Chickpeas), Green Gram / Moong Dal, Black Gram / Urad Dal
  • Millets: Ragi / Finger Millet, Sorghum / Jowar, Pearl Millet / Bajra, Foxtail Millet
  • Grains & Cereals: Rice, Barley, Whole Wheat, Oats, Maize / Corn
  • Nuts: Cashew Nuts, Pistachios, Walnut, Almonds
  • Seeds: Flax Seeds, Sesame Seeds, Cumin Seeds, Fenugreek Seeds, Carom Seeds / Ajwain, Coriander Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Watermelon Seeds, Sunflower Seeds
What macronutrients does Amrot provide?

Proteins from pulses, nuts, and seeds; carbohydrates from grains and millets; dietary fiber from millets, oats, and legumes; healthy fats (omega-3 and omega-6) from nuts and seeds.

Which vitamins and minerals are present?

Vitamins A, B-complex (B1, B2, B3, B6, Folate), C, E, and K; trace Vitamin D. Minerals include Calcium (ragi, sesame, almonds), Iron (chana, ragi, moong, sesame, flax), Magnesium (nuts, seeds, grains), and Zinc (seeds and legumes).
